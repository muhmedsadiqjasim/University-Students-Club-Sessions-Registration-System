# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project aims to provide a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone.

All contributors, users, and visitors are expected to act professionally and respectfully regardless of experience level, gender, age, nationality, disability, religion, or background.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Provide helpful feedback.
- Accept constructive criticism professionally.
- Focus on improving the project.
- Respect different opinions and approaches.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment or discrimination.
- Offensive or abusive language.
- Personal attacks.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in being blocked from participating in the project.
